3. Design and Layout Tips
To make your app look more "modern," focus on layout and organization:
Use Multi-Column Layouts: Streamlit supports multi-column layouts, allowing you to organize content logically and avoid a single, long stream of elements.
Utilize Sidebars and Expanders: Use st.sidebar for navigation or input controls, and st.expander to hide complex details until needed, creating a cleaner interface.
Incorporate Visualizations: Leverage Streamlit's native support for data visualization libraries (like Plotly, Matplotlib, and Altair) to create engaging and interactive charts.
Theming: Experiment with built-in theming options to match a desired aesthetic.
