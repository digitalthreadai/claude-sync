A web application that aggregates weekly deals and offers from multiple grocery stores in a selected city (e.g., Chandler, AZ), pulling promotional data from stores like Fry's, Bashas, and Walmart. Users can create an account, select their city, and build a personalized shopping list of products they're interested in. The app monitors weekly ads from participating stores and sends notifications when those specific products go on sale. It also provides a cost comparison feature that analyzes the user's shopping list against current weekly ads to recommend which store offers the best overall savings for their desired items that week.

Core Features:

City selection with aggregated weekly/daily deals from multiple grocery stores (Fry's, Bashas, Walmart, etc.)
User accounts with personalized product watchlists and notification preferences
Automatic notifications when watched products appear in weekly ads
Cost comparison tool that recommends the best store based on user's product list and current deals
Visual References:
deal aggregators like Flipp, known for their clean presentation of promotional content and product browsing.
